Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LynxFace facial analysis.

Is my photo uploaded to any server?

No, absolutely not. All processing happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly. Your photos never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

How accurate is the facial analysis?

Our AI uses Google's MediaPipe FaceMesh technology, which detects 468 facial landmarks with high precision. The analysis is based on established aesthetic principles and provides scientifically-backed measurements.

What kind of photo should I upload?

For best results, upload a clear, well-lit frontal photo of your face. Make sure your face is centered, looking directly at the camera, with neutral expression and no obstructions.

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! LynxFace works on all modern browsers including mobile devices. The interface is fully responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile experiences.

What metrics does the analysis provide?

You'll receive 20+ detailed metrics including facial symmetry, facial thirds and fifths, golden ratio proportions, feature distances, and an overall attractiveness score based on facial harmony.

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Simply visit our pricing page and select the Premium plan. You'll get unlimited analyses, detailed reports, and priority support for just $9/month.

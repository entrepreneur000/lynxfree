Your Privacy is Protected

All face analysis happens locally in your browser. Your photos never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Information We Collect

LynxFace is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information:

  • No Photo Storage: Photos you upload for analysis are processed entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Contact Form Data: When you submit the contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message through Formspree, a third-party service.
  • Analytics: We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, browser type, etc.) but do not track individual users.

How We Use Your Data

The limited information we collect is used solely for:

  • Face Analysis: All facial analysis is performed locally on your device using MediaPipe technology. No data is sent to external servers.
  • Communication: Contact form submissions are used only to respond to your inquiries.
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous usage statistics help us improve the website experience.

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Processing: All face analysis happens in your browser. Photos are temporarily loaded into memory and discarded when you close the page or reset the analysis.
  • No Server Storage: We do not store, save, or have access to any photos you analyze.
  • Third-Party Services: Contact form data is handled by Formspree, which has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their terms.

Third-Party Services

LynxFace uses the following third-party services:

  • MediaPipe (Google): Face landmark detection runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to Google servers.
  • Formspree: Contact form submissions are processed through Formspree. See their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN: We load styling from a CDN for faster page loads.

Cookies & Tracking

LynxFace does not use cookies for tracking purposes. Any cookies used are strictly functional (e.g., session management) and do not track your personal information.

Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Use LynxFace without providing any personal information (except when contacting us)
  • Request deletion of any contact form data we may have stored
  • Ask questions about our privacy practices

Children's Privacy

LynxFace is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us through our contact form.
